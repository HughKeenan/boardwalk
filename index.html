About Us

Who We Are

The Boardwalk School of Acting is a newly-established acting school based in Co. Carlow. We offer regular lessons to adults and children ages 5 - 18 in dramatic acting and quarterly courses in other aspects of theatre such as singing and dance, direction and use of sound and lighting systems.

Why Join Us

Acting is a fantastic way to meet people and make new friendships for adults and children alike. It offers a chance to try out something completely new and is great for building confidence and helping to overcome fear of public speaking.
