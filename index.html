About Us


Who We Are

The Boardwalk School of Acting is a newly-established acting school based in Co. Carlow. We offer regular lessons to adults and children ages 5 - 18 in dramatic acting and quarterly courses in other aspects of theatre such as singing and dance, direction and use of sound and lighting systems. Actors of all levels are welcome, no prior experience required!

Our team is comprised of 5 industry professionals with a combined 40 years' experience acting on both stage and screen. While not a professional theatre company, in addition to our regular classes, it is our goal to being staging full-length productions and one act plays regularly within the next 2 years, and all members of the group will be able to participate.


Why Join Us

Acting is a fantastic way to meet people and make new friendships for adults and children alike. It offers a chance to try out something completely new and is great for building confidence and helping to overcome a fear of public speaking. It helps with learning how to work in a team and is great for helping to train your memory.

You should consider joining us because of the breadth of experience our team brings to the table. Our staff has experience across multiple different areas, including acting for both stage and film, stuntwork, voice acting and singing. Each member of staff is a qualified teacher for both adults and children.
