About Us

Who We Are

The Boardwalk School of Acting is a newly-established acting school based in Co. Carlow. We offer regular lessons to adults and children ages 5 - 18 in dramatic acting and quarterly courses in other aspects of theatre such as singing and dance, direction and use of sound and lighting systems. Actors of all levels are welcome, no prior experience required!

Why Join Us

Acting is a fantastic way to meet people and make new friendships for adults and children alike. It offers a chance to try out something completely new and is great for building confidence and helping to overcome a fear of public speaking.
