Quarterly Courses


Every quarter we offer a selection of 3 different short courses in areas outside of our normal lesson plan. If you want to expand your skillset or just try something that looks like fun, consider signing up. The same courses won't be repeated every quarter so don't miss your chance! Please note that every class requires a minimum of 5 participants to go ahead, with a maximum capacity of 15, and the cost of the class will be split evenly between the number of attendees.

Here's what's on for Q2 2024:

Musicals Crash Course

Ever dreamed of unleashing your inner Broadway star, or just wished you could sing better in the shower? In this course we will cover the fundamentals of singing and dance, with a vocal coaching session to help you nail your next audition!

Price: €200


Technical Skills for Theatre

If the technical aspect of theatre interests you, don't miss out on this course! It will teach you the use of lighting and sound systems, as well as script reading to help you learn how to best set the scene for your performers.

Price: €150


Stage Combat

Think you're the next Errol Flynn? Well here's your chance to find out. This course will show you a number of aspects of stage combat, including fistfighting, swordplay and guns for stage acting.

Price: €350
